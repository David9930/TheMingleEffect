Break Free from the Digital Cage!

A Site Devoted to Promoting "The Mingle Project's" globally important message

The Mingle Effect was written and produced by Derek Devon for the sole purpose of offering an additional tool to get the word out about Michael Smerconish's "The Mingle Project" message. I was also hoping, it may serve as Michael's warmup song to his, "The Mingle Project's" speaking engagements.

As a long time fan of both his Potus radio show and weekly spots on CNN, I found this one issue was so important to me, it motivated me to write and produce this song. I hope you liked it!

If you believe in "The Mingle Project's" message:
Join the crew, Let's build a future, Strong and true.
Together we rise, hand in hand.
The Mingle Project's here to stand!
