The Mingle Project Message

The Problem

Americans have sorted themselves into increasingly isolated echo chambers, both geographically and digitally. This self-segregation has eroded our capacity for empathy and compromise.

The Prescription

Intentional mingling across social, economic, and political lines. Not forced diversity, but chosen connections that rebuild our social muscles and restore our faith in each other.

The Promise

A renewed America where differences of opinion don't mean demonization of opponents, where political disagreement coexists with personal respect and community bonds.

Why This Matters Now

Michael's message isn't just timely‚Äîit's urgent. As digital natives come of age in an increasingly fragmented society, we're raising a generation that may never learn the fundamental skills of democratic citizenship: listening, compromising, and finding common ground with people who are different from us.
