The Journey to "The Mingle Effect"

The Spark: TC After Dark

Near the end of the Covid pandemic, Derek discovered Teresa "TC" Cannon's podcast "TC After Dark". Episode 79: "How to write a book" changed everything.

"It motivated me to get off my hockey player butt and start writing 'My Book'!"

The Novel: "A Letter Guide For Rebecca"

After 10 months of 12-hour days, Derek completed a 506-page novel. A one-man show doing everything from writing to cover art and layout.

What if you could transfer all your memories into your 12-year-old self?
  • Would you try to prevent 9/11?
  • Save Princess Di?
  • Win the lottery?
  • Win back your high school dream girl?

From Book to Soundtrack

Derek decided to create an album/soundtrack for his book. After all, he'd never written before but managed a novel - why not music?
